Our Story

A Little Place With a Whole Lot of Heart

Pop's Diner started the way most great diners do — with a dream, a cast-iron skillet, and a belief that a good meal can turn anybody's day around. Back in the early days, Pop himself would be here before the sun came up, brewing the first pot of coffee and getting the biscuits in the oven.

Today, Pop's is more than a restaurant — it's where Greensburg gathers. Farmers finishing up early mornings sit next to families spending Saturday together. Regulars who've been coming for decades still claim the same stools, and newcomers are always welcomed like old friends.
