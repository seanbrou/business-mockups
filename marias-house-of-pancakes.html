Our Story

A Family Tradition Since Day One

Maria's House of Pancakes was born from a simple belief: breakfast is more than a meal — it's a warm start to a great day, shared with the people who matter most.

"Every dish we serve is made from scratch, with the same love and care I'd give to my own family sitting around the table."

From fluffy buttermilk stacks to sizzling skillets, we source the finest ingredients and never cut corners. Pull up a chair — we've got a plate waiting for you.
